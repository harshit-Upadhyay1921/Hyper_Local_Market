##  How It Works

1. A business owner registers and creates a shop.
2. The owner adds digital promotional sales.
3. Customers can browse nearby shops within a 10 km radius.
4. If interested, customers generate a QR code for a sale.
5. The shop owner scans the QR to validate and track platform-driven visits.

This ensures accurate tracking of customer acquisition from the platform.
